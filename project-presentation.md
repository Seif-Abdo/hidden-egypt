# Website Idea

The idea comes from an important observation today: many beautiful and unique places in Egypt are still **unknown to most people**.

**Hidden Egypt** is a website that helps people discover **less famous destinations across Egypt**. Instead of focusing on popular tourist spots like the pyramids, the platform highlights **hidden gems**, cultural places, local experiences, and natural locations that deserve more attention.

The website acts as a **simple digital guide** that encourages exploration and promotes **sustainable tourism in lesser-known areas**.

---

# Title

**Hidden Egypt**

The name reflects the main idea of the website: discovering the **hidden beauty of Egypt** that most people have not explored yet.

---

# Services

### 1. Hidden Places Directory

A collection of **lesser-known destinations** in Egypt such as hidden beaches, villages, mountains, deserts, and historical locations.

### 2. Quick Travel Guides

Short guides that include:

* Location
* Best time to visit
* Interesting facts
* What makes the place special

### 3. Travel Inspiration

A section that helps users discover new places by category such as:

* Nature
* Culture
* Adventure
* Relaxation

### 4. Photo Highlights

A visual gallery showing **beautiful images of hidden locations** to inspire visitors.
