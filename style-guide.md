# Hidden Egypt - Style Guide

## Brand Identity
**Name:** Hidden Egypt
**Slogan:** "Discover Egypt Beyond the Famous."
**Concept:** A digital guide that highlights hidden gems, local experiences, and natural locations to promote sustainable tourism in lesser-known areas of Egypt.

## Target Audience
**Age Range:** 18 - 40 years old
**Demographics & Interests:**
* Travel lovers & young explorers
* Students
* Local tourists in Egypt
* International travelers interested in unique discoveries
* People who prefer exploring new, less famous destinations over traditional tourist spots.

## Color Palette
The colors represent nature, exploration, and calm travel experiences.

### Primary Color
**Sand Beige (#E6C79C)**
*Inspired by the Egyptian desert and landscapes.*

### Secondary Color
**Deep Blue (#1E3A5F)**
*Represents the Nile, sea, and trust.*

### Accent Color
**Palm Green (#3A7D44)**
*Represents nature and adventure.*

### Background
**Light Cream (#F9F6F1)**
*Keeps the website clean and elegant.*

## Logo
The brand uses a custom logo (`logo.jpeg`), modified to have a transparent background except for the specific graph area which remains white.

## Services Overview
1. **Hidden Places Directory:** Lesser-known destinations like hidden beaches, villages, and deserts.
2. **Quick Travel Guides:** Short guides with location, best time to visit, and interesting facts.
3. **Travel Inspiration:** Categorized sections for Nature, Culture, Adventure, and Relaxation.
4. **Photo Highlights:** A visual gallery of beautiful hidden locations.
