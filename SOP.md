# Hidden Egypt — Development SOP

Build "Hidden Egypt," an HTML & CSS university project — a travel guide showcasing Egypt's hidden gems.

**Pages:** Home (hero, services, featured places), Explore (place cards with hover effects that show more details about the place, region filter, cards are clickable to redirect to the full place pages), Discover Map (interactive map pinpointing places, links to place pages), Gallery (photo-focused grid, lightbox view, clickable to redirect to the place pages), About Us, Contact Us (form for place suggestions). 

**Place Page:** photos, location, best time to visit, facts, sustainability tips (responsible visiting, support local businesses, environmental notes).
